CCDASTRO Temma ASCOM Driver — Version 1.0.4 Sync Revision

Reason for revision:
The real Temma mount connected correctly but did not respond to the C# driver's
previous Sync command. The working VB6 driver was supplied as the authoritative
reference.

VB6-proven SyncToCoordinates sequence implemented:
1. Validate connected/not parked through the existing driver safeguards.
2. Calculate/use current local sidereal time.
3. Clear serial buffers.
4. Blind transmit T + HHMMSS + CRLF.
5. Wait 100 ms.
6. Blind transmit T + HHMMSS + CRLF again.
7. Wait 100 ms.
8. Blind transmit D + formatted RA + formatted Dec + CRLF.
9. Clear buffers.
10. Wait 100 ms.
11. Update target RA/Dec state.

Important correction:
The prior C# Sync path used a P-based Sync command. The working VB6 driver uses
the T/T/D sequence and does not wait for replies to those commands.

New helper:
SendBlindTemmaCommand() transmits CRLF-framed commands without waiting for a
response, matching VB6 CommandBlind behavior for the Sync initialization steps.
