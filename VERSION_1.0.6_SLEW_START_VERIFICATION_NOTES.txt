Version 1.0.6

Adds non-blocking slew-start verification:
- Saves pre-slew RA/Dec.
- Requires initial R0 from the normal Temma T/P GOTO.
- Returns immediately from SlewToCoordinatesAsync.
- Background worker waits 2.5 seconds and checks reported coordinate movement.
- Retries the complete T/P GOTO sequence if no movement is detected.
- Maximum five accepted GOTO attempts.
- Sends PS and clears Slewing after five failures.
- Handles RA 0h/24h wrap.
- Cancels stale verification on AbortSlew, Park, Disconnect, or a newer slew.
- Logs all verification and retry activity under SlewVerify.
- Does not implement old High Precision Slew; modern clients handle plate-solve centering.
